The white energy visualizes the generative cycle of wu xing.

An element which generates another, is highly ineffective against it.
Ex: Wood is highly ineffective against Fire
An element which is generated by another, is slightly effective against it.
Ex: Earth is slightly effective against Fire

The red energy visualizes the destructive cycle of wu xing. 

An element which destroys another, is highly effective against it.
Ex: Water is highly effective against Fire
An element which is destroyed by another, is slightly ineffective against it.
Ex: Metal is slightly ineffective against Fire.

How effective your element is against your enemies, changes how much damage you inflict.

Highly effective: 120%
Slightly effective: 110%
Neutral: 100%
Slightly ineffective: 90%
Highly ineffective: 80%
